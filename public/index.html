Services

Chiropractic, Physiotherapy and Massage

Chiropractors and Physiotherapists are specialists in the connection of your muscles, bones and joint to the nerves around it. They work closely with our Registered Massage Therapists to get you moving and out of pain.

Acupuncture, LASER, etc.

We have both Traditional Chinese Medicine and Biomedical approaches available. LASER, when used at the right time, accelerates recovery. Cupping boosts your energy and increases muscle recovery.

Orthotics, Stockings, Taping

Give yourself the home care advantage. Our orthotic insoles and compression stocking are custom. Taping can help to accelerate recovery and your return to play.

Nutrition

Our nutrition and supplementation specialists will assess your health holistically and personally guide you through your nutritional plan. We also carry a number of reliable nutritional supplement products.

Our Vision

Knowledge is power

Our practitioners have a wealth of knowledge and a generous heart. They love educating patients on the source of their symptoms and ways to prevent injury from occurring.

Manual therapy is an art

Therapy is most effective if the correct tissue/joint is targeted properly. Our practitioners know the right amount of pressure, the ideal direction and best position with the rest of the body.

Fine Tuning the Body

Many performing artists are perfectionists in their craft. Our practitioners use their skills to help them to optimize a players's movement.

Dr. Martin Lai is a dutifully trained chiropractor, acupuncture provider and kinesiologist specializing in functional ability evaluations, ergonomic testing and developing individualized return to work and return to play programs. Dr. Martin received a degree in kinesiology from the University of Toronto specializing in biomechanics and rehabilitation and a Doctor of Chiropractic from the Canadian Memorial Chiropractic College. In addition, he is certified in Contemporary Medical acupuncture from McMaster University.

Dr. Martin has spent years performing physical testing including: functional assessments, neurological and muscle strength testing for organizations including: CIBC, TD Canada Trust, Metrolinx, State Farm Insurance. Dr. Martin uses the best in evidence to assess for mechanical dysfunctions of the body in order to create personalized treatment plans. His passions lie in getting you moving the way you want.

Dr. Martin is also the chiropractor for X Limit Entertainment, a professional performance team comprised of stunt teams and dancers from multiple disciplines including: martial arts, gymnastics, acrobatics, ballet and other various dance styles. Additionally, Dr. Martin has worked closely with the Canadian Pole Fitness Association as well as the Pole dance for Fitness Instructor Commission and has lectured and workshopped for various pole, acrobatic and circus studios across the GTA. Dr. Martin Lai is passionate about studying different movement patterns and applies his unique perspective on movement patterns to the patients under his care.
