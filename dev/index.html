Physiotherapy

Our physiotherapists utilize therapeutic exercises that correct posture/muscle imbalance, reducing pain, improving body awareness and increasing stamina. These benefits can work for anyone living with a chronic illness to anyone recovering from an injury.

Registered Massage Therapy

Our registered massage therapists treat muscle strains, relieve muscle tension, increase circulation, and promote relaxation. Massage therapy can help deal with postural stress â€“ especially stress that occurs in the shoulders and neck, as well as easing muscle pain by increasing the circulation in the affected area. Massage therapy also has mental health benefits by soothing anxiety and depression through the reduction of stress levels in the body.

Acupuncture

Acupuncture is one of the therapeutic modalities of Traditional Chinese Medicine. Acupuncture can assist with the management of symptoms in conjunction with conventional medical treatment of medical conditions. Our team will assess your condition in relation to the muscular, nervous and skeletal system and use acupuncture to treat the pain â€“ such as neck pain, low back pain and headaches.

Custom Orthotics and Bracing

Custom orthotics helps to reduce stress and strain on your body by bringing your feet back into proper alignment. Custom orthotics with a personalized fit can provide benefits such as faster pain relief, with the common knee and back pain being greatly improved. Another essential benefit is that orthotics work towards supporting the spine and realigning it for proper walking and standing posture.

Motor Vehicle Accident Rehabilitation

Vehicle accidents can result in multiple injuries that require consistent rehabilitation to recover. We offer a range of rehabilitation services to treat common injuries resulting from motor vehicle accidents such as whiplash, back strains, spinal conditions and more. Our therapists provide graduated return to work programs and work hardening programs to get you back to moving the way you want.
