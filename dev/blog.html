Experiment time! The wonders of your feet. Stand up. Focus your vision to a small detail in the distance in front of you. Now try to balance on one foot. Take your mind through what you feel in your toes, through your ankles, and up your shin. Lots of muscles are taking turns activating. Try it on your other foot? Are they equally easy to balance? It's amazing how it works. All 26 bones, 33 joints and over 100 muscles/ligaments/tendons working together just to keep you standing! How did they know when to fire? It's your nerves. Specific nerves that wrap through your muscles/tendons/ligaments and sends signals through your body without you even knowing it. This is proprioception. What happens when you strain a muscle, sprain your ankle or get overworked? Your muscles/ligaments/tendons change their shape. That changes the ability of your nerves to properly analyze where your body is. That puts you at a greater risk of reinjury to even a micro level. There's lots of ways to help you recover faster. Acupuncture. Taping, orthotics, massage, joint manipulation, rehab, nutrition. #nutrition #footpain #chiropractic #movementmedicine #healthyfeet #brokeanail #footpain #walkingfunny #massage #taping #Toronto #footcare #healthy #ticklishfeet #healthfamilies #healthylifestyle #scienceiscool
